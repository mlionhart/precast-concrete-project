We Work Locally

With our new facility, Southern Oregon now has a local precast concrete plan. Instead of going to Eugene or Northern California, you'll be able to get your buried infrastructure needs met here at home. Better turnarounds, better customer service, and better sustainability.

Because of our location, we're able to use locally sourced materials for all of our products. With Table Rock in the background, we can mass-produce manholes or create a specifically designed custom vault, complete with rungs, safety ladders, and shelving. If it can be made, we'll make it in the Rogue Valley

Sustainable Concrete Solutions

We are dedicated to making a product that's not only good for our customers but good for the environment as well. Our Rogue Valley facility earned SMaRT Certification, which means we've taken extra steps to make sure our products and processes are eco-friendly.

This goes beyond using only natural or raw materials and recycling waste material. Our parters were chosen specifically with sustainability in mind. Even if it was as simple as looking for nearby Rogue Valley suppliers to cut down on delivery distances. Some may consider that kind of decision inconsequential - we consider it mandatory

Exclusive Brands

Rogue Valley Precast is the only company in the Pacific Northwest to produce Stone Strong Systems products. These blocks can be made for any job, from landscaping for new residential communities to retaining walls along major thoroughfares. Let us know the measurements and we'll get to work.

We also work with Retain-it and Perfect Base brands for your water retention and sewer system needs. One of the best brands on the market, we keep several Retain-It modules on hand for fast ordering and quick deliveries.
